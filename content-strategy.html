Phase 3

Content Strategy - Frank Pinello

Phase 3 deliverable · Hook bank, Vice repurposing brief, photoshoot brief, IG / YT short-form roadmap.

Premise

Frank's 136K @bestpizza33 audience is engaged and intentional - 50K+ views on process reels confirms the audience wants the craft knowledge, not just the food photos. The content already works. What's missing is a CTA at the end of it. Phase 3 builds a content layer with one job: get the right viewer - indie pizzeria owners and serious craft operators - onto the Best Pizza Checklist opt-in.

Hook Bank - 40 Hooks

Eight categories of operator-pain and craft-curiosity hooks. Each pulls a different segment of the 136K toward the checklist and course.

  1. Why most pizzeria doughs fail before the oven even opens.
  2. The fermentation window I run at Best Pizza that most operators ignore.
  3. The flour switch that changed our crust in three weeks.
  4. Why room temperature matters more than your oven setting.
  5. What I tell every new hire on day one about dough.

Sourcing and Ingredients (5)

  1. The distributor conversation most pizzeria owners never have.
  2. Why your tomato choice is costing you more than your rent.
  3. The San Marzano question. What I actually use and why.
  4. Why I source mozzarella from three different suppliers.
  5. The ingredient swap that raised our margin 11% without raising prices.

Oven Management (5)

  1. The oven calibration most operators do once and forget forever.
  2. Why rotating timing matters more than heat setting on a busy Friday.
  3. What the floor temperature actually tells you.
  4. Three oven mistakes I made in year one at Best Pizza.
  5. The pre-heat window. How long is long enough.

Staffing and Training (5)

  1. The question I ask every pizza-maker candidate in the first five minutes.
  2. Why I lost two good dough guys in a year before I changed this one thing.
  3. The training sequence that gets a new hire to consistent pies in two weeks.
  4. What I tell my team about consistency vs creativity.
  5. The Friday-night setup ritual that protects the whole operation.

Multi-Location Operations (5)

  1. The hardest part of opening a second location that nobody talks about.
  2. Why your systems from location one will break at location two.

Vice Content Repurposing Brief

Current state: Frank has an existing catalog of Vice/Munchies episodes - "The Pizza Show" and related appearances. High production quality, editorial legitimacy, existing audience via Vice's distribution. Zero conversion path for Frank's direct business.

Phase 3 move: With appropriate licensing review, extract 60-90 second clips from existing Vice episodes - process sequences, supplier visits, technique demonstrations. Each clip gets a text overlay CTA: "Learn the full process at [checklist link]" and gets posted to @bestpizza33 and YouTube Shorts. Additionally, build a dedicated YouTube channel for Frank's craft content - longer-form process videos + Vice clip reposts + new behind-the-scenes content from Best Pizza locations.

Why: Zero new filming pressure for the core asset - existing Vice footage does the work. Gives Frank a YouTube presence he owns. Each clip functions as top-of-funnel proof routing to the checklist opt-in.

Photoshoot Brief

Two-day shoot, mid-cycle in Phase 3 (Week 7-8 ideal).

Day 1 - On Location · Best Pizza Williamsburg Kitchen: Frank in working kitchen gear at active Best Pizza location, B-roll of dough process, oven, sourcing delivery, crew training. Natural window light + kitchen practical lighting. 200+ stills, 3-5 hours of process B-roll.

Day 2 - Studio / Counter: Frank behind the pizza counter with finished pies, ingredient mise en place, close-ups of dough and crust. 150+ stills, 2-3 hours of talking-head B-roll for IG cutdowns.

Outputs feed: course landing page, checklist opt-in result page, 40-hook IG content calendar, ad creative library, course launch.

Short-Form Roadmap (90 days)

  • Weeks 4-6: post 5 hooks/week from Categories 1-3 (dough, sourcing, oven) - the highest craft-intent triggers for indie operators
  • Weeks 7-9: post 5/week from Categories 4-6 (staffing, multi-loc, Best Pizza story)
  • Weeks 10-12: post 5/week from Categories 7-8 (Vice repurposing, indie operator bridge) + retargeting ads layered on top of organic

Cadence: 5 IG reels + 5 YT shorts + 1 long-form / week. Repurpose ratio 1:5 - one filmed asset = one long-form YouTube video + 5 short cuts.

Conversion Goal

Every piece of content has the same final CTA: "Get the Best Pizza Five-Point Checklist - sourcing, dough, oven, staffing, margin. Free at [link in bio]" - with the bio link routing to the checklist opt-in page.
