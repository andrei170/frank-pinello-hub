Phase 1+2

Email Sequences - Frank Pinello

Phase 1 + 2 deliverable · Pre-call nurture (3 touches), post-call (4 touches over 14 days), 10-day welcome flow.

Sequence 1 · Welcome Flow (10 days, 8 touches)

Triggered when a new subscriber downloads the Best Pizza Five-Point Checklist. Segmented by opt-in tag (indie pizzeria owner vs pizza enthusiast).

T+0 - Immediate (Checklist Delivery)

Subject: Your Best Pizza Five-Point Checklist - plus one thing most operators miss Body: Checklist delivery, 1-paragraph context on how Frank uses these five variables across locations, link to download PDF, soft note about the course launching soon. Goal: deliver the promise, set expectations for what comes next.

T+1 day - The Dough Story

Subject: The fermentation mistake I made for two years at Best Pizza Body: A specific production mistake Frank made early at Williamsburg - fermentation timing, hydration ratio, a supplier decision that cost margin. How he fixed it. Closes with "the course is the documented version of what we figured out." Goal: build credibility through admitted mistakes, bridge checklist to course.

T+3 days - The Sourcing Story

Subject: The tomato conversation that changed Best Pizza Body: Specific sourcing story - a supplier switch, a distribution decision, a cost-per-pie calculation. Concrete numbers. CTA toward Best Pizza Operator Course landing. Goal: prove operational depth beyond the restaurant brand.

T+5 days - The Staffing Story

Subject: Why I lost two good dough guys in the same year Body: The retention and training system rewrite. What Frank changed at location two that he didn't do at location one. CTA toward course landing. Goal: build trust through the multi-location proof - shows Frank ran the experiment.

T+7 days - The Vice Bridge

Subject: What the cameras didn't show at Best Pizza Body: One thing from the Vice/Munchies filming process that didn't make the final cut - a process detail, a sourcing decision, an operations moment. The course is where that full picture lives. CTA toward course. Goal: use Vice authority as proof, bridge editorial credibility to course buyer frame.

T+9 days - Soft Pitch Open

Subject: Quick question before I disappear from your inbox Body: Two-line check-in. "Is the Best Pizza Operator Course a fit for where you're at?" Link to landing. "Or hit reply and tell me what's actually broken in your operation." Goal: segment hot vs warm; route hot to landing, warm to a 1:1 reply for triage.

T+10 days - Last Touch (segmented)

  • Indie pizzeria owner: Last call for $497 standard tier. CTA toward course landing with specific module callout relevant to their tagged bottleneck.
  • Pizza enthusiast: "The course is built for operators. If you ever open a location - come back and we'll talk. For now, here's the Vice episode playlist." Routes to YouTube.

Sequence 2 · Pre-Call Nurture (3 touches before discovery call)

Triggered when a course buyer applies for the optional 1:1 strategy add-on or when someone inquires about the Best Pizza Community.

T-7 days

Subject: Before our call - three things I'll want to know about your operation Body: The three questions Frank will ask - current volume, biggest consistency problem, staffing structure. Helps the operator arrive with real numbers.

T-3 days

Subject: What I learned from operators in year two versus year one Body: Story-driven. One specific insight about what changes between a one-location operator and a two-location operator. Sets the room for the call.

T-1 day

Subject: Tomorrow at [time] - one thing to have ready Body: Calendar reminder, one specific prep ask (last week's food cost %, current hire status, a dough consistency problem they're working on).

Sequence 3 · Post-Call Follow-Up (4 touches over 14 days)

Triggered after the discovery call regardless of outcome.

T+1 hour - Recap

Subject: From our call + two things to try this week Body: 1-paragraph recap, 2 specific action items from the call. Link to the agreed-upon next step (course / community / wait list).

T+3 days - The Specific Resource

Subject: That [dough / sourcing / staffing] thing I mentioned on the call Body: Whatever specific resource was promised - a cost-per-pie calculation template, a supplier question list, a hiring criteria sheet. Sent as a clean PDF.

T+7 days - The Check-In

Subject: Did you try [X] yet? Body: Soft accountability nudge. "Reply with a one-line update if you ran it. Reply with a question if you hit a wall."

T+14 days - The Decision

Subject: Where you landed Body: Clean ask: "Are we moving forward with [agreed path] or holding off?" No pressure. Clear decision frame. Routes to course enrolment or long-term nurture accordingly.

Subject Line Bank (rotation)

For ongoing broadcasts beyond the welcome sequence:

  • "What I'd do differently opening Best Pizza today"
  • "The sourcing call I have every Tuesday"
  • "Why Fridays are a margin test"
  • "Three numbers I look at every morning"
  • "The hire I almost missed"
  • "What the Vice shoot taught me about process"
  • "Consistency without chaos"

Triggers + Tags

  • Tag by opt-in type on checklist download (indie-operator / enthusiast)
  • Tag by purchase (Course $497 / $997 / Community application)
  • Tag by content engagement (opened 5+ in 30 days = "warm operator")
  • Auto-suppress promotional during launch week (replace with launch-specific track)
  • Re-engagement campaign at 60 days of inactivity with a new process reel as the re-entry hook
